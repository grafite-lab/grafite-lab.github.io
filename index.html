Grafite Lab

Engineering is taking an idea out of your head and making it work in the physical world. It should be something that has to survive contact with gravity, heat, tolerances and your own mistakes. That friction is what makes engineering beautiful.

Aim is to help people who are building outside of software and CS, because those require both resources and time. We want to make sure your well researched ideas get turned into prototypes, and you get in touch with industry veterans dealing with the same, at a much larger scale.

Selection is two in-person conversations, wherever you want that to happen.

We trust you before you've proven anything, and we don't ask for anything grandiose in return. The only thing we ask is that you stick with it when it stops working and you don't know why. We will handle the funds and the human connections you might need.
