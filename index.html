Grafite Lab

Engineering is taking an idea out of your head and making it work in the physical world. It should be something that has to survive contact with gravity, heat, tolerances and your own mistakes. That friction is what makes engineering beautiful.

We're a club for people building outside of software and CS, because those require both resources and time. We work together to make sure your (most Indian undergrads pitching "innovative" ideas don't actually know what SOTA is, so they think they're at the frontier when they're not) ideas get turned into prototypes, and you get in touch with industry veterans dealing with the same, at a much larger scale.

Selection is two in-person conversations, wherever you want that to happen.

We trust you before you've proven anything, and we don't ask for anything grandiose in return. The only thing we ask is that you stick with it when it stops working and you don't know why.
